Personal weight tracking iOS App

I found that the apps that were on the app store for weight tracking were not exactly what I wanted. Bodybuilding.com has a cluttered app, while StrongLifts is too specific to the StrongLifts program. I chose to make this app as a personal project, because I thought it would be a great learning experience as well as something that I could actually use.

Skills used: Apple iOS Programming, Swift 3.0, Core Data Stack
